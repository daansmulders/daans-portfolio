GLP-1 medications like Ozempic and Wegovy require weeks of titration and close monitoring. Yet care coordination between patient and doctor is still fragmented — patients track progress in notes apps, doctors get little signal between appointments.

I wanted to explore what a dedicated tool could look like. But more than the domain, I was interested in a question about process: how much of a well-designed, working product could I deliver by treating AI as a genuine collaborator — not just a code generator?

This is the result: a two-sided prototype for patients and doctors, built end-to-end using **Claude Code** and a structured spec-to-implementation workflow called **SpecKit**.
