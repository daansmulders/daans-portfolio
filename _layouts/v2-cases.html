Daan Smulders is a UX and digital product designer based in The Hague, The Netherlands.

For the past 10+ years, he has designed digital experiences for platforms in finance, sustainability, marketplaces and retail. He turns complex journeys into human-friendly products and enjoys bringing positive energy to the teams he works with.

Previously at: funda, Ace & Tate, Werkspot, Powerly

Currently at: Nationale-Nederlanden
